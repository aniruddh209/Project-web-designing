About India

India is a land of incredible diversity. From the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas to the serene backwaters of Kerala, every corner tells a unique story.

With over 1.4 billion people, India is the world's largest democracy and one of the oldest civilizations with a rich cultural heritage spanning more than 4,500 years.

Famous Landmarks

Taj Mahal

The iconic white marble mausoleum in Agra, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Qutub Minar

The tallest brick minaret in the world at 73 meters, built in 1193, showcasing exquisite Indo-Islamic architecture.

Cultural Heritage

Classical Dance Forms

India has eight classical dance forms including Bharatanatyam, Kathak, and Odissi, each with unique styles and expressions.

Musical Heritage

From classical Hindustani and Carnatic music to diverse folk traditions, India's musical heritage is vast and varied.

Festivals

Celebrations like Diwali, Holi, Eid, and Christmas showcase India's religious diversity and cultural richness.
