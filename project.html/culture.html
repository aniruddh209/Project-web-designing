Indian Festivals

India's festivals are a spectacular display of its cultural diversity and religious harmony.

Diwali

The festival of lights symbolizing the victory of light over darkness.

Holi

The colorful festival celebrating love and the arrival of spring.

Eid

Marked by prayers, feasts and charity, celebrating harmony.

Christmas

Celebrating the birth of Jesus Christ with joy and festivities.
